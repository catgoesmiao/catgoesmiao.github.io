project description

Of course, none of them will get what they want…
…and all of them will pay dearly for their choices.

Tachyon Chasing is an in-development interactable visual novel-esque literary experiment. The two person team behind this project consists of one artist and one writer. They recommend you follow along with the development process if you enjoy:

  • cold showers, fundamental lack
  • rejection of teleology and technocratic progress
  • smelly unkempt protagonists with self-annihilatory limerence
  • snooping around emails and documents that arent yours
  • the rupturing of historical continuums
